Procedure Education

General Surgery

Watch: Your Surgical Procedure Explained

Procedure Overview

  • Your surgeon will explain the specific steps of your operation prior to the procedure.
  • General anesthesia or regional anesthesia may be used depending on your case.
  • The surgical team includes a surgeon, anesthesiologist, and scrub nurse.
  • You will be monitored throughout the procedure with vital sign equipment.

Risks & Considerations

  • All surgical procedures carry some risk, including infection, bleeding, or anesthesia reactions.
  • Your medical team has assessed your individual risk profile and taken steps to minimize complications.
  • Please inform your team of all current medications, allergies, and prior surgical history.

Recovery Expectations

  • You will be taken to a recovery room immediately after surgery where your vitals are monitored.
  • Pain management will be provided. Report any discomfort promptly to your nurse.
  • Follow all discharge instructions regarding diet, activity restrictions, and follow-up appointments.
  • Recovery time varies by procedure and individual health. Your team will provide a personalised timeline.

King Abdulaziz University Hospital
Ministry of Education — Jeddah, Saudi Arabia

Informed Consent for Surgical Procedure

I, the undersigned patient, hereby provide my informed consent to undergo the scheduled surgical procedure at King Abdulaziz University Hospital (KAUH). I confirm the following:

1. Nature of the Procedure

I have been informed about the nature, purpose, and objectives of the scheduled procedure. I have had the opportunity to ask questions and receive satisfactory answers.

2. Risks and Benefits

I understand that all medical and surgical procedures involve some degree of risk. The potential risks, complications, and benefits of the procedure have been explained to me in a language I comprehend.

3. Alternatives

I have been informed of available alternative treatments, including the option of no treatment, and their potential outcomes.

4. Confidentiality

I consent to the use of my clinical data for treatment purposes, with full assurance that my personal health information will be kept confidential in accordance with applicable laws and hospital policy.

5. Right to Withdraw

I understand that I have the right to withdraw my consent at any time before the procedure commences, without prejudice to my continued care.
